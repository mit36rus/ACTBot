ACTBot is a grid bot for trading on crypto exchanges 

Binance.com and ByBit.com with the possibility of hedging orders.

The main feature of the ACTBot trading bot is the opening of orders with the specified step and coefficient according to the RSI signal, making transactions at the time of the trend reversal according to the RSI, averaging assets to close the transaction in a plus, as well as the use of stop loss.	

One of the latest innovations in the ACTBot bot is two-way trading on futures with the possibility of making a profit both by lowering the price and by increasing it.	

As well as a new futures trading strategy. ByBit.com by hedging orders. Use order hedging to minimize liquidation.

An example of the ACTBot bot using the calculation of the ACTBot RSI SHOOT indicator: 

This indicator is a calculation of two different indicators of the "Relative Strength Index". 

The first value, "Length low", is responsible for a short period, and "Length high" is responsible for a longer period of time. The conditions for opening long trades will be the intersection of the short line * from bottom to top of the long line** , as well as the mandatory value of "Long Shoot" and "Short Shoot" , this value should not be more than at the moment the calculation of the long line. For example, the yellow line has a value of 7 and crosses the blue line with a value of 24 from bottom to top at a level below 30. To calculate the opening of the shorts, the value of the long line should not be lower than that specified in the "Short Shoot" parameters. This indicator calculates almost ideal values for opening trades and averaging them.
